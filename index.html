Panda


The giant panda was known as black and white cat-footed animal

  1. Giant panda also known as panda bear or simply panda, is a bear native to south central China.
  2. is easily recognized by the large, distinctive black patches around its eyes, over the ears, and across its round body.
  3. The name "giant panda" is sometimes used to distinguish it from the unrelated red panda

sloth



Sloths are arboreal mammals noted for slowness

  • The sloth is so named because of its very low metabolism and deliberate movements
  • Sloths are almost helpless on the ground, but are able to swim
  • They are classified in the order Pilosa along with the anteaters

The Folivora is divided into five families

  1. Three-toed sloth, which contains four extant species
  2. The most common is the brown-throated sloth which inhabits the neotropical ecozone[1][8] in the forests of South and Central America.
  3. The pale-throated sloth, which inhabits tropical rainforests in northern South America
  4. The maned sloth now found only in the Atlantic Forest of southeastern Brazil.
  5. The critically endangered pygmy three-toed sloth which is endemic to the small island of Isla Escudo de Veraguas off the coast of Panama.

Otter


Otters are carnivorous mammals in the subfamily Lutrinae

  1. The word otter derives from the Old English word otor or oter.
  2. An otter's den is called a holt or couch.
  3. The feces of otters are typically identified by their distinctive aroma
